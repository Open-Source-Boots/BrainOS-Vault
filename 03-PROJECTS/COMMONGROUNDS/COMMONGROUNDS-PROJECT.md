# 🌱 CommonGrounds — Project File

CommonGrounds is the commercialized layer of BrainOS: a local-first AI system packaged for DSPs, caregivers, and creators who want HIPAA-compatible second-brain infrastructure without cloud dependency or subscription lock-in.

> **The core pitch:** You built a system for yourself that works. Now you teach others to build it. The vault IS the product.

---

## What CommonGrounds Is

BrainOS is the engine. CommonGrounds is how the engine gets into other people's hands.

The system targets a deeply underserved audience: direct support professionals, caregivers, and small-team operators who are drowning in documentation, compliance, and context-switching — and who cannot afford or cannot legally use cloud AI tools that send protected health information to external servers.

Local LLMs running on a home desktop are the **only** HIPAA-compliant path for this audience. You know this because you live it. That's the moat.

## Business Architecture (3 Layers)

| Layer | Platform | What It Does | Revenue Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Learn in public** | YouTube (BrayDonDon) | Document building BrainOS; attract audience organically; establish authority | Indirect — feeds other layers |
| **Sell the system** | Skool community | Paid community + BrainOS starter kit + monthly challenges | Recurring MRR |
| **Power the system** | Shopify / Gumroad | Packaged digital tools, templates, setup guides | One-time product sales |

**Why the layers stack:**
- YouTube builds trust before the ask
- Skool converts trust into recurring revenue
- Digital products capture people who don't want a community — lower friction, lower price point

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Direct Support Professionals (DSPs), family caregivers, PFT providers, group home operators
- Shared pain: documentation burden, compliance anxiety, time poverty
- Underserved by AI tools due to PHI concerns
- Deeply loyal when someone speaks their language — Brayden speaks it fluently

**Secondary:** Creators and solo operators who want a structured second brain without cloud subscriptions
- Obsidian-curious users who can't figure out how to make it actually work
- People already interested in local AI but overwhelmed by setup

## Why This Has a Moat

1. **Lived experience** — 4 years as a DSP/PFT — you know the forms, the audits, the iLink system, the inspection anxiety
2. **Already built** — the vault exists and works; most people selling systems don't use one
3. **Local AI compliance story** — PHI never leaves your hardware; this is a real, defensible claim
4. **Price gap** — enterprise HIPAA-compliant AI platforms charge $50K+/year; CommonGrounds targets $49–$97/month
5. **Timing** — local LLMs crossed the usability threshold in 2025–2026; first-mover window is now
