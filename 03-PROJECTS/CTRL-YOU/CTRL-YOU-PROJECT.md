# CTRL+YOU

## Overview
CTRL+YOU is Brayden's digital empowerment brand. The core thesis: teach everyday people to take control of their tools, their data, their systems, and their time — using open-source and self-hostable technology where possible.

Note: The folder is named `CTRL-YOU` in Obsidian due to `+` syntax conflicts, but the brand name is **CTRL+YOU**.

## Brand Pillars
- **Control** — Own your tools, own your data
- **Accessibility** — Free and open-source first
- **Practicality** — Real workflows, not theory
- **Identity** — You are the system architect of your own life
