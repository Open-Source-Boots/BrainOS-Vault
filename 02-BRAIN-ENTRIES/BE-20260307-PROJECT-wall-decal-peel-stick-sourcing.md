# Brain Entry — Kray Studios Wall Decal & Removable Wallpaper Sourcing Research (Mar 7, 2026)

## CONFIRMED FACTS
Only facts explicitly stated in this thread. Nothing inferred.

- Kray Studios targets Gen Z renters aged 16–28 who cannot paint walls | stated in thread as core demographic | Master Context File + thread response
- Removable peel-and-stick wall decals are confirmed to exist as a dropshippable product category | stated in AI response | thread response 1
- CJ Dropshipping carries wall decals and peel-and-stick wallpaper options | stated in AI response citing CJ blog | thread response 1
- CJ Dropshipping sourcing costs for basic decals start as low as $0.71 per unit | stated in AI response | thread response 1
- Retail price range for self-adhesive wall sticker packs on Amazon: $11–$22 | stated in AI response | thread response 1
- Peel-and-stick wallpaper rolls can be sourced at $3–$8 and retailed for $18–$30 | stated in AI response | thread response 1
- Bundle Slot 2 in the Kray Studios bundle structure is designated as the "vibe anchor" slot | referenced from store master file | thread response 1
- Wall decals fit Bundle Slot 2 as the primary vibe-setting product | stated in AI response | thread response 1
- TikTok content around "renter-friendly decor" pulls 100K+ likes routinely | stated in AI response | thread response 1
- Solid-color peel-and-stick wallpaper panels are applied in vertical strips typically 24" wide | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Adhesive on peel-and-stick panels is pressure-sensitive — grips wall but releases cleanly | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Smooth walls work best for adhesion; heavily textured walls reduce adhesion | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Full accent wall typically takes 4–6 panels and a couple of hours solo | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- A company called **Peel & Paper** makes a product called "Sarla Plain Solid Color Wallpaper" — available in custom colors, fully removable | stated in AI response citing peelandpaper.com | thread response 2
- Peel & Paper offers three material types: Peel & Stick Paper (matte), Peel & Stick Vinyl (waterproof/wipeable), and Paste-the-Wall (stronger hold, still removable) | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Solid-color peel-and-stick wallpaper is harder to dropship at low cost compared to patterned decals | stated as caveat in AI response | thread response 2
- CJ Dropshipping primarily carries patterned decals and wallpaper — plain solid-color panels are less common in their catalog | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Specialty suppliers for solid-color panels include: Peel & Paper, Tempaper, WallBlush — these do not have traditional dropship programs | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- For solid-color sourcing on CJ, recommended search terms: "black self adhesive wallpaper," faux linen texture, faux plaster/limewash effect | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Faux-texture options (linen, plaster, limewash) look like solid color but have visual depth and photograph well for TikTok content | stated in AI response | thread response 2
- Brayden described the concept he had in mind as "almost like Fatheads but a whole wall or room" | stated by Brayden | thread query 2
