## 📋 AI Strategy — What Actually Works

Based on research (2026 sources):

- **Highest ROI AI uses for individuals:** First-draft writing, research synthesis, meeting/note summaries, scheduling/time-blocking, data pattern recognition
- **Time savings:** Tasks taking ~90 min without AI accelerated ~80% with Claude-class models
- **Key risk:** AI intensifies work — freed time gets refilled with more tasks; need intentional "AI practice" with breaks and limits
- **Best approach:** Map weekly work → find 2–3 high-time/low-creativity tasks → apply AI there first
- **Collapse the latency** between intention and execution: pre-load context, template decisions, shrink iteration cycles from days to hours
