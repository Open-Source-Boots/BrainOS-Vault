\#\# What Youtube Content Is

Kray Studios is Brayden's creative umbrella brand / parent company.  
It encompasses all content, creative, and business projects.  
Ctrl+You is the first revenue-generating arm under this brand.

\#\# YouTube Channel Concept

Channel thesis: Kray Studios exists to make interesting videos about what it feels like to be young and trying to understand how the world actually works while the rules keep changing.

This is NOT a generic finance or advice channel.  
The channel is about authentic experience: labor, money, ADHD, creative work, figuring it out.  
Target: young people in similar situations to Brayden.

Content style: honest, direct, first-person, low-production but high-authenticity  
Format tested: YouTube Shorts primary format (worked from phone \+ laptop while at work)  
Sprint constraint: content must be doable at work, on phone, without hours of free time

\#\# Channel Identity (developed in March 2026 sessions)

\- Voice: "young person figuring out how the world actually works"  
\- Anti-fluff: no aspirational hustle content, no fake wins  
\- Themes: work, money, ADHD, labor rights, creative projects, open-source ideas  
\- Tone: conversational, unpolished is fine, real over perfect
